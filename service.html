Our Services

Ipo-Lab (Pty) Ltd diagnostic and services capabilities will cover the Agriculture sector where customers will be assisted with tests on soil, water, livestock and crop diseases. Our strategy involves taking the laboratory and supplies services to the people through a mobile laboratory for sampling, analysis and data creation. .

HEALTH

Ipo-Lab (Pty) Ltd will be the leading supplier of health services in the diagnostic market. We will ensure this happens through research of the current global and local trends in medical developments in novel and common diseases prevalence. This will ensure that we are always ahead of our competitors in bringing solutions as demanded by the current needs. Through research, we will ensure that we become a reference laboratory to carry out tests for disease that are a threat to the nation. This will be achieved by ensuring that we are equipped with cutting edge testing equipment and development of protocols that will be able to release results in desirable turnaround times.

ENVIRONMENT

Ipo-Lab (Pty) Ltd will equip itself to be able to perform tests not only for livestock and people but also for wildlife diseases, water bodies in rivers and Wastewater Treatment Plants. Sustainable development goals call for a sound Waste management. Through our relentless innovation, waste will be recycled, re-used and disposed of in an environmentally sound manner.
